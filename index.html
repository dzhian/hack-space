Yuyan Li

As an artist, my practice is an intricate dance between virtual and physical realms, a symbiosis of computer modelling and tangible sculpture. It is an exploration of the mutable boundaries of reality, where technology and tradition intertwine, creating a space for dialogue that provokes social introspection.

My approach is heavily influenced by Social Practice Theory, believing art to be more than an aesthetic expression, it is a powerful social instrument. I weave intricate narratives of societal structures and norms into my works, turning each piece into a three-dimensional discourse that invites viewers to reflect upon, question, and even reshape their social surroundings.My art does not exist to be merely observed but to provoke interaction. The spectator becomes a participant, a vital catalyst that transforms the static into the dynamic. Through this shared experience, my art initiates a dialogue, prompting a collective reimagining of our societal construct, and positioning art as an active player in shaping our shared reality.
