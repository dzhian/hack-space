The history of Underwear
Making Underwear
Interaction experiments

about

Through the deconstruction of the history of underwear and data collection, our group built a responsive interactive drawing board using p5js and presented our critique and practice of the symbolic symbol of underwear by building a static webpage, aiming to provoke the general public to think about the social connotations metaphorized by this consumer product, and a reflection on women's bondage to idle social signifier.
